What is the dress code?

We kindly ask our guests to dress in formal wedding attire. The ceremony will take place in a church, so we suggest elegant and appropriate outfits to celebrate this special day with us.

What will the weather be like?

Vasto in late July enjoys warm and sunny summer weather. Expect temperatures around 28-32°C (82-90°F) with minimal chances of rain. We recommend light fabrics and comfortable shoes for the evening celebration.
