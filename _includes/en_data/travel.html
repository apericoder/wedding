By Plane

The closest airport is Pescara International Airport (PSR), about 75 km from Vasto. From there, take a Trenitalia train to Vasto-San Salvo or rent a car. Alternatively, you can fly into Rome (FCO or CIA) and take a Di Fonzo bus directly to Vasto.

By Car

Vasto is accessible via the A14 (Autostrada Adriatica). Take the exit at "Vasto Nord" or "Vasto Sud" depending on your location. Parking is available near the venue and in the city. Get Directions.

By Train

The main train station is Vasto-San Salvo, served by Trenitalia. You can reach it from Pescara, Rome, Bologna, or Milan. Once there, taxis and buses are available to reach the venue.
